3d printed bulbs are designed to snap onto the original brass sockets that come
with each lamp. LEDs are wrapped around the upper part of the bulb and secured
with silicone and clear gorilla tape. The 3 LED wires use the hole in the
housing and should be sealed with silicone to weatherproof the lamps

It can optionally be held onto the socket with a DIN-965 M3 x 0.5 x 8mm set
screw. This will prevent the bulb from releasing from the socket

The diffuser is an optional part to help hide the LEDs for lamps with very
sheer lampshades. It is secured to the bulb with an DIN-965 countersunk
screw: M4 x 0.7 x 10mm

These items are printed upside down and without supports. Align the Z-seam
opposite to the lower screw hole for the bulb and to the nearest upright on
the diffuser
