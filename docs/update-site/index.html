Install LampOS

Instructions:

  1. Unplug your lamp's cord from any battery packs or USB chargers before starting
  2. Plug your board into a USB slot with a USB micro cable and download the driver
  3. Connect using the button below. Once connected, hold down the tiny boot button on your board
  4. While holding the button, choose upload Install LampOS and check erase
  5. Your board will start to initialize. Once finished, disconnect the USB micro cable
  6. Reassemble your lamp and ensure it boots

Once your lamp has started up, you can connect to "standard-lamp" on WiFi to configure the lamp. Once connected, visit: http://192.168.4.1 to setup colors and behaviors

Beta channel

Warning: Beta builds are rolling, unstable, and untested. They may contain bugs, break OTA, or fail to boot. Only install if you're comfortable reflashing your lamp over USB to recover.
